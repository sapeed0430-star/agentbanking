# ICP Segments + Pricing One-Pager (Draft)

## Product Statement
AgentBanking issues tamper-evident Receipt/Certificate artifacts for autonomous investment agent activity, combining cryptographic signing, trusted timestamping, and transparency-log proof bundles to support audit and risk-review workflows.

## ICP Segment 1: Quant Startup / Emerging Fund

- Team size: 5-30
- Pain: fast iteration, weak governance evidence, investor trust gaps
- Buying trigger: LP due diligence and first institutional onboarding
- Needed features: API-first verification, downloadable receipts, lightweight dashboard
- Price sensitivity: high
- Buyer outcome message: "Show investors a clear evidence trail (request -> report -> receipt) without slowing strategy iteration."

## ICP Segment 2: Mid-Market Asset Manager / Fintech Desk

- Team size: 30-200
- Pain: fragmented controls, manual audit preparation, incident response delays
- Buying trigger: recurring audit burden and risk committee pressure
- Needed features: policy versioning, evidence vault, reviewer workflow, exports
- Price sensitivity: medium
- Buyer outcome message: "Reduce manual audit packaging time by issuing standardized Receipt/Certificate bundles for each verification event."

## ICP Segment 3: Regulated Broker/Dealer or Exchange-Integrated Platform

- Team size: 200+
- Pain: strict recordkeeping, legal hold, external examiner scrutiny
- Buying trigger: regulatory remediation programs and control modernization
- Needed features: immutable retention, dedicated environment, SIEM integration, strict SLA
- Price sensitivity: low, but procurement rigor is high
- Buyer outcome message: "Strengthen control evidence consistency across compliance, legal, and incident response teams."

## Overage and Add-ons

1. Verification overage: tiered per 10k events.
2. Additional storage: per GB-month.
3. Dedicated tenancy and BYOK: enterprise add-on.
4. Compliance export pack: per framework per month.
